Smash Club was founded by Todd Patterson with the idea of having a place to play super smash bros ultimate. While many people who has join Smash club do know how to play not all future members will know how to play the game in the most efficent way. So this site was created to help beginning players to better understand the game and what strategies are the best.
Character stats will hold information about the different characters and their moveset. Facts will hold information about characters world and the style of fight that they use.
